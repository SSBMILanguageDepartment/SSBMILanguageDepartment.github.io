Native American Heritage Night 2025

Séekas’in! Thank you for joining the Shingle Springs Band of Miwok Indians for Native American Heritage Night 2025 at the Golden 1 Center on the Tribe's ancestral Nisenan homelands which are now encompassed by the City of Sacramento.

We are the SSBMI Environmental Department, Language Department, and Traditional Ecological Knowledge (TEK) Department. You can learn about the work we do, both in Sacramento and throughout the Tribe's broader homelands, by clicking the links below to access info pamphlets we have provided.

Esak’ahá daak’ábe mi c’aykɨ́ɨ? (Do you want to know more?)

This website is managed by the SSBMI Language Department to host resources for learning the Nisenan and Northern Sierra Miwok languages. This includes various language resources that we have developed by working in collaboration with the Environmental and TEK departments. You can learn more about these languages and the work we do to promote and revitalize them by checking out the rest of this website.

If you have questions about the Language Department, please contact us at language@ssband.org.

If you have questions about the Environmental Department, please contact mrtayaba@ssband.org.

If you have questions about the TEK Department, please contact cpettis@ssband.org.
