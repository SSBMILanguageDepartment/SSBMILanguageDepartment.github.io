Earth Day 2024

The SSBMI Language Department created stickers that say "Let's take care of the earth!" in Nisenan and Northern Sierra Miwok for Earth Day 2024. Here, you can learn how to say the language on these stickers, which we encourage you to share with others!

K’awí sitapaypé!

Miwok

If you would like a copy of our Earth Day 2024 sticker, please visit the SSBMI Language Department in the SSBMI Tribal Administration building or contact us at language@ssband.org. Supplies are limited.
