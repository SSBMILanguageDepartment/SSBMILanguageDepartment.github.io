Do you want to know more?

Check out the rest of the website to find other learning resources, such as language flashcards and games. For example, we also have printable animal flashcards and a "Go Fish!" game which you can use to practice animal names in Nisenan and Northern Sierra Miwok. You can also check out our plant signage webpage to learn more plant names in Nisenan and learn about their traditional uses.

If you have questions, please visit our offices in the SSBMI Tribal Administration building or contact us at language@ssband.org.
