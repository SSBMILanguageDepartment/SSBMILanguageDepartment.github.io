Menekɨ́m siyéet’obe hedém website-í? (Who made this website?)

This website was created by SSBMI Language Department staff in order to share Nisenan and Miwok language learning resources with the Tribal Membership and other learners. Our current team consists of: Jonathan Geary, Richenda Ervin, and Skye Anderson. The Nisenan and Miwok audio recordings that you will find on this website are recordings of Jonathan, Richenda, and Skye. We are all learners of the Nisenan and Miwok languages, and we hope that you will join us in learning Nisenan and Miwok too!

The SSBMI Language Department is part of the Cultural Resources Division of the Shingle Springs Band of Miwok Indians. We host regular Nisenan and Miwok language classes and other language-learning events, develop Nisenan and Miwok learning resources (e.g. language games, books, and self-paced learning materials), and share Nisenan and Miwok language information via other means such as an ongoing word-of-the-day campaign on social media (please follow the Cultural Resources Division on Facebook!). If you would like to know more about our efforts, or if you have any questions about the Nisenan and/or Miwok languages, please contact us at Language@ssband.org.
