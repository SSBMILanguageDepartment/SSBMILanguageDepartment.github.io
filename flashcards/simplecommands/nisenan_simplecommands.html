# Nisenan English
01a Hatíp! Stop it!

(said to 1 person)
01b Hatiwá! Stop it!

(said to 2+ people)
02a Wallíp! Be careful!

(said to 1 person)
02b Walliwá! Be careful!

(said to 2+ people)
02b Walliwá! Be careful! (said to 1 person)
11a Henamén! Don't yell!

(said to 1 person)
11a Henamén Don't yell! (said to 1 person)Note: The verb hena- means 'to yell'. When you add -men to the end, it means 'to not yell'.
11b Henamenwá! Don't yell!

(said to 2+ people)

(said to 1 person)
