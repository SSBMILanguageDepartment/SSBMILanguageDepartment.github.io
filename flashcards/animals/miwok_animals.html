The Flashcards

001 - Bobcat

﻿Tóllomma

'Bobcat'

Heṭéeyɨs tóllommay aa?

'Do you see the bobcat?'

Mínto tóllomma’?

'Where's the bobcat?'

'Mountain Lion'

'Look at the mountain lion!'

Híiliica’ ɨwɨ́ɨ.

'The mountain lion is eating.'
